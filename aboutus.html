About us

This Manchester United Fan Club was started by an avid die hard fan of the club and this site is managed by a small group who love this club. We support the team during all the times, the highs and the lows. We are trying to connect with more such type of person who share the same feeling as we have for the club. We try to give the latest and quickest updates and hot transfer news getting the information from reliable sources. You will get newsletter from us on your email if you subscribe to us. There will special events like fancams and fun events and interviews with the players themselves.
